DUNCAN HARRIST MUSIC

Duncan Harrist is a multi-instrumentalist/singer-songwriter who has been heating up the already-spicy Austin music scene since 2015.

01. Heartbreak Car Crash


Loud, raucous and raw, “Heartbreak Car Crash” is the sonic equivalent of a head-on collision and the first single from his upcoming debut album. The album’s songs are funny, honest, and stylistically diverse, featuring musical hooks and lyrics guaranteed to worm their way from the listener’s earholes directly to the brain and heart.  

Associated Acts

As a member of the Josh Koons band, he has contributed complex rhythm guitar, memorable leads, vocal harmonies, and occasional wry commentary performing at such venues as:

  • Spider House Ballroom
  • Ginnie’s Little Longhorn Saloon
  • One-2-One
  • Solstice Fest 2017
  • Sahara Lounge
